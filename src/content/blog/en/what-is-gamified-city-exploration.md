Do you walk the same streets every day without really *seeing* any of them? Gamified city exploration is an answer to exactly that problem: it turns the city into a playground with quests to complete, points to earn and badges to unlock.

## Why does gamification work?

Gamification means bringing game mechanics — quests, points, levels, badges, collections — into non-game contexts. The streak counter in your language app and the daily goal in your step tracker work on the same principle.

These mechanics press three powerful psychological buttons:

1. **A sense of progress:** every completed quest creates visible advancement. Our brains love closing open loops (the Zeigarnik effect).
2. **Curiosity of discovery:** "what's behind this pin?" turns an ordinary walk into an expedition.
3. **The urge to collect:** badges and collections turn experiences into a tangible archive — a photo album you can play.

## What changes when you apply it to a city?

Classic city tourism runs on *lists*: top 10 sights, most famous museums. You finish the list, take the photos, and your bond with the city stays shallow.

Gamified exploration treats the city as a *map full of possibilities* instead:

- **The route is yours:** quests are scattered across the map; the order is up to you.
- **Hidden gems surface:** not just tourist landmarks — a craft workshop, a neighborhood viewpoint or a local food spot can be a quest too.
- **Replayability:** new quests keep appearing even in your home city. "Exploring" stops being something only tourists do.

## Who is it for?

- **Locals** who want a concrete reason to break their routine and rediscover their own city.
- **Travelers** who want a playful answer to "where do I even start?" in a new city.
- **Groups of friends** — leaderboards and shared quests make exploring social.
- **Anyone building a walking habit** — more fun than a step goal.

## How to get started

No equipment needed; a phone and comfortable shoes will do. The principle is simple:

1. See the quests around you on a map,
2. Pick one and walk to its location,
3. Check in while you're there and collect your reward.
